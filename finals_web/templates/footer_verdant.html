Verdant
Home & Garden Learning Marketplace

About

Verdant is an educational e-commerce project that simulates a home and garden marketplace. It is designed to help students practice real-world web development concepts while exploring how digital platforms can support greener, more sustainable living.

This site showcases core marketplace flows — browsing products, managing carts and orders, messaging between buyers, sellers, and riders, and handling notifications — all within a sandbox environment where no real payments or deliveries take place.
