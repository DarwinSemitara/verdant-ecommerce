Need help with Verdant?

Using Verdant as a User

  • Creating an account: Click Sign up on the login card, accept the terms, choose Sign up as User, then complete your personal details and password.
  • Setting up your profile: After signing up, go to your account/profile page to add your address, contact number, and (optionally) a profile photo so riders can find you more easily.
  • Browsing products: Use the home page categories, search bar, and featured sections to discover items. Click a product card to see full details, photos, and seller information.
  • Managing your cart: From any product page, add items to your cart. In the cart you can adjust quantities, remove items, and see an updated subtotal before checking out.
  • Placing an order: On checkout, confirm your delivery address, payment method, and review the final total. When you submit, separate orders are created per seller so each store can manage its own part.
  • Tracking status: Open your Orders page to see each order's status (pending, accepted, with rider, delivered). The timeline shows where you are in the process.
  • After delivery: Once your order is marked delivered, you can review items, contact the seller for concerns, or place a new order using previously saved details.

If you forget your password, use the Forgot password link on the login card and follow the reset steps to regain access.

Using Verdant as a Seller

  • Applying as a seller: From Sign up, choose Sign up as Seller and provide your basic info, store name, and contact details. Make sure the email and phone are active.
  • Approval process: An admin reviews your application for completeness and basic compliance. While waiting, you can still log in but some features may be limited until approval.
  • Setting up your catalog: In the seller dashboard, add products with clear names, descriptions, prices, and photos. Group items into categories so customers can find them easily.
  • Handling new orders: When a customer checks out with your products, the order appears on your My Orders page. Review items and totals, then accept or reject with a reason if needed.
  • Preparing items: After accepting an order, prepare it for pickup. Make sure quantities and packaging match what the user ordered to avoid issues during delivery.
  • Working with riders: Once accepted, your order becomes visible to riders. They handle pickup and delivery, while you keep inventory and product information up to date.
  • Customer communication: If something is out of stock or needs substitution, update the order or reach out via the platform's messaging and notifications, depending on your workflow.

Sellers focus on maintaining accurate listings, honoring policies, and preparing orders on time. Riders and the system handle the final delivery to customers.

Using Verdant as a Rider

  • Applying as a rider: From Sign up, choose Apply as Rider and submit your contact details, address, vehicle type, and ID photo. This helps the team verify your identity and eligibility.
  • Onboarding & approval: After submitting, an admin reviews your application. You can still log in and access the Rider Dashboard while pending, but you can only accept real deliveries once approved.
  • Setting your service area: On your first login, an onboarding overlay walks you through pinning your main delivery area on the map. Choose a place you can regularly serve (e.g., your city or nearby barangays).
  • Finding orders: In the Available Orders tab, you see orders from nearby sellers that match your service area. Check the total amount, address, and list of items before accepting.
  • Accepting & delivering: When you accept a delivery, the order moves into your Current Deliveries tab. Pick up items from the seller, follow the displayed address, and update the delivery status as you progress.
  • Updating status: Use the rider controls (e.g., out for delivery, delivered) so the customer and seller are kept informed. Completed deliveries appear in Delivery History for your records.
  • Staying organized: Keep your contact details and service area updated so Verdant can continue matching you with nearby orders that fit your schedule.

While your application is pending, you can freely explore the dashboard, practice using the map, and familiarize yourself with how orders flow from seller to customer.
